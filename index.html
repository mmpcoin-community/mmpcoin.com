What is MMPCOIN($MMP) ?

Roadmap

MMPCoin is a community-driven open-source satire project aimed at exploring the innovative possibilities of cryptocurrency. We do not have a fixed roadmap, maintaining flexibility to keep pace with the rapidly changing market environment.

MMPCoin does not have a dedicated development team, which means that every participant is free to contribute their ideas and creativity. We encourage community members to actively engage in discussions and experiments, but it is important to clarify that MMPCoin does not endorse any form of investment.

Our goal is to create an open and inclusive experimental platform, welcoming everyone interested in cryptocurrency to join us in exploring future possibilities together.
